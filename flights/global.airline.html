AIRLINE
NAME : GLOBAL
MODEL : 7500

More than 150 of Bombardier’s Global 7500 ultra-long-range jet are now in service, five years after it achieved type certification. We took another look at how multiple elements of the cabin design support passenger comfort on long flights, including one of the best-equipped galleys on the market, the floating-base Nuage seats, and the separate entertainment zone and bedroom. And there’s more to come in terms of range and speed from the upcoming Global 8000.

CAPACITY: 40 TO 50 MEMBERS
